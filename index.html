Who Am I

I graduated from the Ogle School of Hair and Design in 2004. I am also licensed and trained in lashes, color as well as hair-extensions. I completed an internship at a local Salon, also in Grapevine before I moved to running my own business at Phenix Salon.

I have been happily married to my husband for almost 2 years. I also have a wonderful step-daughter. I have been playing Roller Derby for 9yrs on the Wrecking Crew, a team in the Dallas Derby Devils Roller Derby League.

What I do Exactly

Cut

I begin with a complete consultation and customized cut. Finished with a invigorating shampoo and scalp massage with conditioning products. Blow dry and style included with every service.

Color

I have been educated by the Rusk Color line. If you want pink hair, I can do that. Hi-lights? Easy. Anything you can think of. I love being able to use color creativity with my clients as my work shows.

Hair Extensions

I am fully trained and educated by the Cinderella hair line. You will get a full consultation on what works best for you. I can apply tape-in as well as hot-fusion extension. I only use the best, high-quality hair.

Special Occasions

This is a favorite of mine. I enjoy having a role in making my clients feel beautiful on their special day (whatever it may be). Whether it’s prom, Wedding Day, or any other special occasion.
